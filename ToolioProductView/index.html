Frequently Asked Questions

What is TweetDeleter?

TweetDeleter is a specialized tool designed to help you manage and clean your Twitter timeline. It allows you to bulk delete tweets, filter content, and maintain a professional social media presence with advanced search and deletion capabilities.

How does tweet deletion work?

TweetDeleter works in three simple steps:

  • Connect your Twitter account securely through OAuth
  • Search and filter tweets based on date, content, or engagement
  • Select and delete tweets individually or in bulk

Is there a limit to how many tweets I can delete?

The limits depend on your plan:

  • Free Plan: Up to 3 tweets per day
  • Premium Plan: Unlimited deletions
  • Business Plan: Unlimited deletions across multiple accounts
  • Lifetime Plan: Unlimited deletions with one-time payment

Can I recover deleted tweets?

No, tweet deletion is permanent. We recommend using our archive feature to backup important tweets before deletion. Twitter's own archive can also be downloaded before using our service.

Is TweetDeleter safe to use?

Yes, TweetDeleter is completely safe:

  • Uses secure OAuth authentication
  • Never stores your Twitter password
  • Complies with Twitter's API guidelines
  • Includes data encryption and privacy protection

Can I schedule automatic deletions?

Yes, premium users can set up automatic deletion rules based on:

  • Tweet age
  • Keyword filters
  • Engagement metrics
  • Custom schedules

What happens to my media attachments?

When you delete a tweet, any associated media (images, videos) will also be removed from your timeline. We recommend backing up any important media before deletion.
